About Me


Desdemona Wren is an LGBTQIA+ novelist who writes gay fiction with monsters, witches, and actual plots. She's from Seattle, WA, but currently resides in the mystical land of Northern California with her boyfriend and their two cats. Where everybody wears coats all the time, nobody says 'brah', 'bruh', 'bro' or any variation of that word, and absolutely nobody surfs.

Her two cats are named Oliver and Ophelia and they have traveled the world. From The Great Smoky Mountains, to the tallest peaks of The Cascades, and even to the Grand Canyon; they've been everywhere.
